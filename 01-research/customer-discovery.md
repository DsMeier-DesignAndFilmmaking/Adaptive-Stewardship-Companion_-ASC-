# Customer Discovery

## Purpose

Track raw interview notes and synthesized findings from conversations with prospective customers across candidate segments.

## Key Questions to Answer

- What operational decisions do they currently struggle with?
- What tools/spreadsheets/processes do they use today?
- What would they actually pay for, and who signs off?
- What's the recurring pain vs. a one-off complaint?
